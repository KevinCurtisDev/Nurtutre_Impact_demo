Learning Outcomes

What participants will gain from this programme

✓

Build a Strong Governance Foundation
Understand the key principles, structures, and responsibilities that underpin effective governance within Irish community, voluntary, and social enterprise organisations.

✓

Strengthen Oversight & Accountability
Apply practical frameworks to ensure transparent decisionmaking, robust controls, and strong accountability to funders, regulators, and the communities they serve.

✓

Enhance Strategic Leadership
Confidently engage in strategic planning, performance monitoring, and impact measurement to support longterm organisational sustainability.

✓

Improve Board Culture & Dynamics
Foster constructive relationships, inclusive discussions, and psychologically safe board environments that enhance collective leadership and effectiveness.

✓

Apply Governance in Real-World Scenarios
Navigate complex governance challenges—such as conflicts of interest, safeguarding concerns, HR issues, and crisis situations—using evidence-based approaches.

✓

Strengthen Financial Governance Competence
Interpret financial information, understand funding conditions, and implement appropriate oversight of financial risk and compliance.

✓

Embed Risk & Compliance Practices
Develop and maintain risk registers, safeguarding policies, GDPR awareness, and compliance calendars tailored to their organisation’s needs.

✓

Lead Effective Meetings & Decision-Making
Use structured decisionmaking tools, effective chairing techniques, and improved meeting practices to increase clarity, efficiency, and follow-through.

✓

Develop Practical Governance Systems
Create usable policies, procedures, and governance manuals that support consistent, well-documented organisational practice.

✓

Champion a Culture of Good Governance
Promote ethical practice, transparency, continuous improvement, and values-based leadership throughout their organisation.
