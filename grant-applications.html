Grant Application Masterclass Programme

Leverage Our Expertise To Build Funding-Ready Applications

VIEW MODULES VIEW PRICING

Structured Training for Funding Success

The Grant Application & Business Plan Masterclass Programme is designed to build the capacity of community groups, social enterprises, SICAP target groups, LEADER project promoters, and voluntary boards seeking funding.

Each session includes branded materials, participant handouts, practical templates aligned with Pobal and funder expectations, real-world examples, and follow-up resources.

View Programme Modules

Who Is This For?

Designed for organisations at every stage of their funding journey

Community Groups

LDC-supported groups, voluntary boards, and committees preparing funding applications.

Social Enterprises

Trading organisations seeking LEADER, Rethink Ireland, or other social enterprise funding.

SICAP & LEADER

Target groups and project promoters working with Local Development Companies.

Programme Modules

Module 1

Understanding the Funder & Appraisal Mindset

How funders assess applications, what appraisers look for, and how to avoid common pitfalls.

Module 2

Writing a Strong Needs Analysis

Building an evidence-based case using local data, consultation, and funder-aligned rationale.

Module 3

Designing Activities That Score Highly

Turning needs into clear, fundable, deliverable actions.

Module 4

Building a Transparent, Defensible Budget

Eligible costs, value for money, cost realism, and linking budgets to activities.

Module 5

Writing Outcomes, Outputs & Impact Statements

How to demonstrate change, measure success, and align with programme indicators.

Module 6

Governance, Capacity & Risk

How to present organisational strength, track record, and risk mitigation.

Module 7

Writing a Business Plan for Social Enterprises

A Pobal-aligned structure covering market need, financial projections, and social impact.

Module 8

The Appraisal Lens: How Applications Are Reviewed

A high-level, ethical explanation of how appraisers assess clarity, consistency, feasibility, and evidence.

Module 9

Writing for Appraisers: Style, Structure & Clarity

How to write in a way that improves readability and scoring.

Module 10

Final Review & Submission Checklist

A step-by-step quality assurance process used by strong applicants.
